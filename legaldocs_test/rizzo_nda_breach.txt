Case No. 2025‑RAT‑INF‑09

Company v. Rizzo “The Squeak” Tailman

Criminal Indictment – Breach of Non‑Disclosure of Cheese‑Related Information

IN THE MUNICIPAL COURT OF SEWER DISTRICT NO. 4

Charges

The Defendant, Rizzo Tailman (alias: “The Squeak”), is charged with:

Breach of section 4(a) of the Cheesetech Industrial Nondisclosure Agreement, wherein Defendant was granted access to proprietary information including, but not limited to:

Cheese types in development,

Aging timelines,

New crumb‑retention technologies.

On or about 3 November 2025, Defendant was observed communicating confidential cheese matters to non‑authorized rodents at a local alley behind “Three‑Day‑Old Pizza & Co.”

Surveillance indicates that Defendant stated:

“Psst, cheddar batch is coming out stronger this quarter.”

Requested Relief

The Prosecution seeks:

Maximum penalty of community service in cheese lab sanitation,

Revocation of cheese tasting privileges,

Mandatory tail‑etiquette training.

Submitted on behalf of Cheesetech Industries Legal Office
